Will Standley

Hi, I’m Will Standley!

I’m 21 years old and have lived in Crystal Lake, Illinois, my whole life. I went to Crystal Lake South High School and graduated in 2021. During high school, I was a multi-sport athlete. I played football in the fall as a running back and ran short-distance sprints for the track team in the spring. In the winter, I was the student manager for the basketball team. After graduating, I went to McHenry County College, where I earned my Associate in Science in 2023. Now, I’m going back to school part-time, working towards a career in coding and programming.

For the past year, I’ve worked full-time at Cellular Sales, an authorized Verizon retailer, as a store trainer. I love the energy at work. It’s competitive, but we have a lot of fun with it. We often have contests where we can win things like gift cards, team outings, or even special dinners with our regional directors. Shortly after starting there, I met my gym partner, Rey. We’ve been consistently going to the gym together for about a year now, keeping each other motivated.

In my free time, I enjoy spending time with my girlfriend, Gil. One of my favorite memories was when we visited Chicago on two separate occasions. The first time, we toured the Shedd Aquarium and walked along the boardwalk. Another time, we visited Navy Pier and unexpectedly got to watch fireworks during a festival. Both days were filled with great moments and fun surprises!
