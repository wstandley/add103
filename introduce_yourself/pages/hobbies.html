Hobbies

Gaming

One of my favorite hobbies is gaming, and I spend a lot of time playing a variety of games. My favorite games, in order, are:

  1. Stardew Valley
  2. Pokémon
  3. Baldur's Gate
  4. Madden
I play most of my games on a PC that I built about two years ago, which has a 3060 Ti graphics card, a B550 motherboard, and 32GB of DDR4 RAM. I also play on an Xbox Series S and a Nintendo Switch, which is themed after Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom.

I've been playing Pokémon for as long as I can remember. It’s a game I grew up with, along with other Nintendo classics, and it has a special place in my heart. I was introduced to Stardew Valley about three or four years ago, and it’s a game I never seem to get tired of. I’ve spent countless hours on it, always finding something new and enjoyable.

Recently, I started playing Baldur's Gate, which has been an awesome experience. It’s the first D&D-style game I’ve played, and I’m really enjoying the gameplay and how immersive it feels. As for Madden, it’s a game I grew up playing with my brother. While I’ve never been into the competitive modes like head-to-head or Ultimate Team, I still enjoy playing it casually from time to time.
