Gaming

In my free time, I like to play games on both my Xbox and the PC I built myself. My PC has an ROG motherboard and a Nvidia 3060 Ti graphics card, which works great for gaming. I enjoy upgrading it, and I plan to keep making it better as time goes on. I mostly play sandbox and RPG games because they let me explore and try different things. Some of my favorite games are Stardew Valley and Animal Crossing, where I can build, farm, and relax. I also love playing Pokémon and Baldur's Gate 3, which have more story and strategy. Gaming helps me relax and have fun after a long day. It’s great having both a console and a PC because I can play a lot of different games depending on my mood. As I keep upgrading my PC, I’m excited to see what new games I’ll be able to play.

Cars

I got into cars when I was 17, and I've loved them ever since. One of my favorite things to do is go to car shows and meets, where I can see all kinds of cool cars and talk to other people who like cars too. About three years ago, I bought a 1986 Pontiac Fiero, and it's been a fun project for me to work on. I really enjoy taking care of it and making little upgrades here and there to keep it running well. I also own a 2020 Mazda CX5, which I bought last spring. It's the car I use every day, and I like how smooth and reliable it is. A few years ago, I even worked on cars for a job, and though I don't do it for work anymore, I still love spending time working on my own cars whenever I can.

Anime

In my free time, I really enjoy watching anime, especially when I’m getting ready to go to bed. It’s nice to relax and watch a few episodes before falling asleep. My favorite animes so far are Attack on Titan and Naruto, including both the first series and Shippuden. I love the exciting battles and the strong characters in these shows, which always keep me interested. When I’m just sitting around with nothing to do, anime is a fun way to pass the time. Whether I’m watching old episodes of Naruto or catching up on Attack on Titan, it’s a great way to relax. I like how these shows have both action and emotional moments. Watching anime helps me unwind, especially when I have some free time or want to relax before bed.

Gym

Going to the gym has become a big part of my life over the past year. I make sure to go five days a week, working out at both LA Fitness and Edge Fitness. I stay dedicated to getting stronger and healthier, and it’s something I really enjoy. Whether I’m lifting weights, doing cardio, or trying new exercises, I always push myself to improve. Along with working out, I’ve started learning how to cook and meal prep. My favorite dish to make is grilled chicken with curry, which is both healthy and tasty. By preparing my meals, I ensure I have good food ready to support my workouts. This combination of exercise and eating right helps me stay on track with my fitness goals while also teaching me new skills in the kitchen.

Work

I work at Cellular Sales, an authorized retailer for Verizon. As a store trainer, I get to help my team grow and learn new skills, which is something I really enjoy. Being part of a great company like Verizon allows me to connect with customers every day and help them with their needs. One of my favorite parts of the job is helping clients find new phones that I know they’ll enjoy. It’s rewarding to make sure they leave the store happy and excited about their new devices!
